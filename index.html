Emonix Research Project - University of Wisconsin Madison
(Sep 2014 - Aug 2015)

Emonix is a home automation and energy sustainability research project. During my time working on Emonix I developed web and mobile dashboards, backend infastructure and demos for competitions. This past spring Emonix won 3rd place in the Wisconsin Energy and Sustainability Challenge. Emonix is a temperature control system that lets the user control which rooms are heated and cooled while the system learns the heating and cooling trends of the user and makes decisions to save energy. Another sustainability project I worked on that stems from Emonix is a water softener monitoring system that is installed in dorms across the UW Madison campus and alerts personnel when it is optimal to add water softener salt. This system saves the University of Wisconsin Madison more that $2,000 in salt per semester per dorm!

Software Engineer - Badger Loop

I have taken a lead role in developing the software and hadware that will be used for the networking module that will be recording and sending metrics to our Mogodb database. I am also designing a front end console using Meteor and a mobile app for viewing live pod metrics!
